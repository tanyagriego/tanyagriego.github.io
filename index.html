Do some cats lay eggs?

No. Cats do not lay eggs. They are mammals, and MOST mammals do not lay eggs, the only exception are the 5 species of monotremes, which include the Platypus and 4 species of Echidnas.
Catbirds lay eggs, but they are birds.
